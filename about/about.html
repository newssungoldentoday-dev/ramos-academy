About us

Education shouldn't have a price tag.

Ramos Academy is a free learning platform built on one idea: anyone, anywhere, should be able to learn anything — regardless of what they can pay.

What we believe

01

Free, always

No paywalls, no premium tier for basic learning. Every lesson is free.

02

Self-paced

No deadlines, no cohorts. Learn as fast or as slow as you need.

03

Locally relevant

Curricula adapted to how each country actually teaches its students.

Want to help?

Ramos Academy is an open project. Contributions, corrections, and new lessons are always welcome.
