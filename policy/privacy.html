Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and learning progress (courses viewed, exercises completed). We do not collect payment information because Ramos Academy is free.

2. How we use it

  • To save your progress across sessions
  • To recommend relevant lessons and subjects
  • To send account-related emails (password resets, important updates)

3. What we don't do

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not show ads. We do not share your learning history with anyone outside Ramos Academy.

4. Cookies

We use minimal cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, ask us to correct it, or delete your account at any time by contacting us.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via our contact page.
